Deflationary Tokenomics

The Lazer Eyes community is rewarded through deflationary tokenomics.
Every member of Lazer Eyes will be able to claim their reward for being a part of this amazing project! The rewards are given out as tokens from our deflationary economic system, where each token has an inherent value that increases over time so people have incentive not only to buy and hold them but also continue providing value in return.

Helping the Blind

Lazer Eyes believes in giving back. As such, LZR is committed to donating 100% of its transaction fee to charities and non-profits helping the blind. These donations are made through a charity every month that helps people with sight impairment achieve their best vision possible for whatever they do everyday. This includes making sure children get glasses or other eye care when needed so life can be better for them too!
