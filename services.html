Hold on for dear life

HODL is a philosophy that stands for "Hold on to dear life". It was used by cryptocurrency users in the early days of Bitcoin when they were unsure if their currency would be worth anything. HODLers are patient buyers who think long-term, believing that all crypto will eventually find their true value - even if its takes months or years. These people believe cryptocurrencies will become mainstream. HODLers don't sell off at lows, like most “paper hands” do, because this causes downward momentum which leads prices lower than what's warranted by fundamentals as seen with BTC during 2018. This group believes you should ignore short term losses while holding onto your crypto over time.
